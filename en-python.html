Python in a nutshell

Python is very versatile and adaptive, but at the same time a very logical programming language. Thats why it fits so well for a coder newbie, but also for a coder guru with tens of years of experience. You can make lots of different programs with Python, for example 3D-printer scripts, a variety of complex calculators and simple games or parts of bigger games.

The trustworthy Python-coder of JavaGalaksi is Toivo, who has made many kinds of Python programs for years. I will be adding from time to time some of Toivo's creations, but if you want to know more about his software, go to his public MediaFire -folders here.

There is one bad thing with Python: it hasn't the ability of making applets or similar, what means it isn't easy to get Python -programs to the vast world of Internet. Well, it is SOMEHOW possible, but unfortunately our service provider doesn't support that method.

So, you have to download Python itself from here, and remember to load v. 2.7.2, because Toivo's programs aren't compatible with the 3.xx -versions. When you have downloaded and installed Python, download some of Toivo's or anybody else's programs(for ex. leetspeak.py), and open it up at Python IDLE. When you have done that, press F5 to run the program. It might be a bit complicated, but it works!
